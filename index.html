Chicken Pepperoni

At MJ Restrone, our Chicken Pepperoni is crafted for those who love bold flavors and satisfying bites. Made from quality chicken and a carefully selected blend of spices, this delicious favorite delivers a perfect balance of smokiness, mild heat, and savory taste. Every slice is prepared with attention to freshness and flavor, making it a versatile choice for pizzas, sandwiches, wraps, and snacks.

Our Chicken Pepperoni is juicy on the inside, lightly crisp when cooked, and packed with seasoning that enhances every dish it’s added to. Whether layered on a pizza, tucked into a sandwich, or enjoyed on its own, it reflects our commitment to quality, taste, and consistency. At MJ Restrone, we believe great food starts with simple ingredients and careful preparation—and our Chicken Pepperoni is a perfect example of that philosophy.
